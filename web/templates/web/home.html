ABOUT POLE PERFORMANCE

Pole Performance is a pole dance competition based in Scotland and is open to anyone. The Watermelon Studio and Carousel Fitness wanted to create a competition where the routine focused on the overall performance and not just the difficulty of tricks.

We wanted to create a competition where each routine was a true performance, from the choreography right down to the costumes. Our first competition ran in March 2016 which was a huge success and we hope to run for many more years.

ORGANISERS

     

The Watermelon Studio

The Watermelon Studio specialises in Pole Fitness/Dance in Central Edinburgh. We offer Pole and Flexibility classes weekly and several workshops throughout the year. We also host dance parties ranging from Pole Dancing to Twerking and much more!

   

Carousel Fitness

Carousel Fitness is a studio based in Inverkeithing that runs a variety of alternative fitness classes. From Pole to HIIT, Burlesque to Stretch and Flex, our aim as a school is to increase fitness, boost confidence and teach new skills in a fun and fresh way.
