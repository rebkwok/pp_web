ABOUT POLE PERFORMANCE

Pole Performance is a pole dance competition based in Scotland. The Watermelon Studio and Carousel Fitness wanted to give pole dancers of all levels a chance to display a routine with flair and passion.

After last year's success, we are looking forward to another talent-filled event in 2017.

JUDGES & COMPÈRE



Tiff Finney - Compère

Tiff has performed for thousand of people whether it be at Club Noir or on Britain's Got Talent. As well as her amazing performances, workshops, and being an ambassador for Bad Kitty, Tiff also owns her own gorgeous studio called Pole Physique. And of course she was United Pole Artists "Bringing Sexy Back" Poster Model 2016. We are very excited to have her join us again as our compère for Pole Performance 2017!




Jess Leanne Norris

Self taught Jess starting her incredible pole journey aged just 14 years old. Jess owns and runs her own dance and fitness studio JLN.

Miss Pole Dance UK winner 2011 and 2015 as well as award for best tricks.

We are super excited to have Jess and her expertise on our judging panel for Pole Performance 2017.


Charlotte Robertson

Charlotte began pole around 4 years ago & fell in love with it! She started competing around 3 years ago & her titles include:

  • Miss Pole Dance Doubles Champion 2013
  • MPD Uk 2nd place
  • UKPPC Professional Champion 2014
  • World Championships finalist 2015
  • UKPPC Elite Champion 2015
  • PCS Asia 2016- 2nd place

Charlotte is also the owner of Girls Love Fit Pole & Aerial Fitness & Xpole ambassador. She is also very lucky to have taught her workshops around the world & loves being able to share her passion for pole dancing with her students!


Michael Donohoe

Co owner of Fierce fitness Ireland, Michael has 10 years' experience within the fitness industry. Michael recently competed at the World Pole and Fitness championship were he placed 4th, an amazing result.

  • All Ireland pole dance champion 2013
  • Irish Pole Fitness male champion 2013
  • Pole Theatre Ireland professional winner 2014
  • World Pole Dance Sport and Fitness finalist 2015
  • Mr Pole Dance UK winner 2015

With an abundance of experience, we are excited to have Michael on our judging panel!


ORGANISERS

     

The Watermelon Studio

The Watermelon Studio specialises in Pole Fitness/Dance in Central Edinburgh. We offer Pole and Flexibility classes weekly and several workshops throughout the year. We also host dance parties ranging from Pole Dancing to Twerking and much more!

   

Carousel Fitness

Carousel Fitness is a studio based in Inverkeithing that runs a variety of alternative fitness classes. From Pole to HIIT, Burlesque to Stretch and Flex, our aim as a school is to increase fitness, boost confidence and teach new skills in a fun and fresh way.
