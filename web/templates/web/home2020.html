ABOUT POLE PERFORMANCE

Pole Performance is a pole dance competition based in Scotland. Carousel Fitness want to give pole dancers of all levels a chance to displsay a routine with flair and passion.

JUDGES




Crystal Gibson - Finals Judge

Crystal is a mother, wife, a pole studio owner and instructor from Los Angeles & Seattle, USA. In 2016 she moved to the UK with her husband and daughter and went on to be the founder and owner of Fly Girls Fitness.

Crystal has taught pole artistry workshops worldwide, and in countless online videos with Cleo's Rock-N-Pole as an online instructor. Recently travelling to Australia to be a guest instructor at Shimmy & Maddie’s PDA’s Summer Pole Camp 2019.

Crystal says her biggest pole accomplishments has been in a group setting, while performing for Leigh Ann Reilly’s "Pole Show LA" in both L.A. and Las Vegas. Crystal spent 4 years as a principal dancer at "Girl Next Door Show" (where in 2014 she won Performance of the Year for her "Inside my Love" routine) & "SEVEN", both pole and aerial shows in Los Angeles.

Crystal became a certified judge for the IPSF in 2013 and has sat on the judging panel of some of the most high profile pole competitions in the world.


Felicity Logan - Finals Judge

Felicity Logan is a Scottish born pole dancer who is known for her extreme flexibility and distinct sensual style. She traveled the world as an exotic dancer and has always stayed true to her sexy roots.

She is also a self taught contortionist and loves nothing more than helping her students reach their flexibility goals.

She has many titles under her belt including Pole Theater Sydney 2017, Dance Filthy Australia 2016, Miss Pole Dance Australia Doubles 2017, Pole Theater Classique winner 2015 just to name a few.
